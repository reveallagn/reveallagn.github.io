ReveaLLAGN

Revealing Low-Luminosity AGN

Overview People Data Publications

Overview

ReveaLLAGN is a Cycle 1 JWST program to get the 1.6-28 micron spectra of 7 nearby low-luminosity active galactic nuclei with MIRI/MRS and the NIRSpec/IFU.
